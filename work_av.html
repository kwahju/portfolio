Work details

Perhaps one of the most powerful component of a multimedia website, these two projects are part of our Audio-Visual class.

Both projects are co-produced with my classmate, Livio Capalbo. Hardware used: HD video camera and external microphone. Software: SoundtrackPro and FinalCut Pro.

The first project is a video promotion that we did for a fictional travel series Vancouver 234 where each episode highlights a different Vancouver neighbourhood. In this episode, it is Commercial Drive.

The second video is for Ginga, a fictional Brazilian mountain bike company.
