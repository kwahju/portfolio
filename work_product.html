CD Cover, Menu and Media Kit

Work details

I chose the theme Celebrate Vancouver to showcase the diversity of our city. A spectrum of colours dominate the CD cover. For the inside of the cover. the spectrum continues on in the background. Here, there is a brief introduction of each celebration.

For the menu, we go the Far East. The exotic spices and the promise of romantic sojourn is accentuated with the restaurant's name, Monsoon, and the picture of herbs and spices highlighting the menu.

The third project is part of a media kit for a local Spanish-language newspaper. The idea is to showcase covers of previous publication with social media component added. Design and colours are kept lively and bright following their colour scheme.

Software used: Adobe Photoshop and Adobe InDesign.
